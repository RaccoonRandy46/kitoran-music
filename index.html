Who I am

My name is kitoran. I'm a musician and cartoon artist. I don't like to share much about my personal life, but I love to share my creative work with the world!

I began making music in January 2020, and since then I have created 54 songs and 5 albums.

I have been using FL Studio to create my music since October 2023, but I started off using a free app on my phone called Medly, which I used to create my first three albums.
